Our Story

Three Decades of
Automotive Prestige

From a single showroom in Mombasa to six locations across East Africa and Dubai — Mater Dealership has been synonymous with quality, trust, and the finest imported vehicles since 1989.

Who We Are

Kenya's Most Prestigious Dealership

Mater Dealership was founded in 1989 with a simple mission: bring the world's best vehicles to the roads of East Africa at honest prices. We import directly from Japan and the United Kingdom, ensuring every vehicle meets strict quality standards before arriving at our showrooms.

We deal in brand new cars, quality used imports, commercial vehicles, buses, trucks, motorbikes, jetskis, and agricultural equipment. Whether you're looking for a reliable daily runner or a head-turning luxury SUV, Mater has it.

Our team of experienced advisors, logistics specialists, and certified mechanics work together so that every customer leaves with confidence in their purchase — and long after.
